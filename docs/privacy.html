Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Daily W ("we", "our", or "the app") is a motivational message app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is stored and used.

We do not sell your data. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

1. Anonymous Authentication

Daily W signs you in automatically using Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This creates a randomly generated user ID (UID) for your session without requiring any account, email, or password.

  • What is stored: a random UID (e.g. abc123xyz).
  • What is NOT stored: name, email, phone number, or any personal identifier.
  • Purpose: to save your preferences (notification times, tone, favorites) across app sessions and devices.

If you uninstall the app, your anonymous account and associated data are eventually deleted from Firebase.

2. Data We Store in Firestore

We use Google Cloud Firestore to store your in-app preferences and usage data, linked to your anonymous UID. This data includes:

  • Your chosen notification times (morning, afternoon, evening).
  • Your tone preference (e.g. sarcastic, gentle, hype).
  • Optional nickname (chosen by you, can be left blank).
  • IDs of messages you have favourited.
  • IDs of messages you have seen (for rotation — so you don't see the same message twice).
  • Your current streak count and last-opened date.
  • Your FCM push notification token (see Section 3).
  • Whether your account has an active Daily W Pro subscription (true/false).
  • Your device timezone offset in minutes.

None of this data contains personally identifiable information. It is used solely to operate the app's features.

3. Push Notification Tokens (FCM)

To deliver your daily motivational messages as push notifications, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). When you grant notification permission, your device's FCM token is stored in Firestore linked to your anonymous UID.

  • What it is: an opaque device identifier generated by Google, used only to route push notifications to your device.
  • What it is NOT: your phone number, email, or any personal contact detail.
  • How it is used: our Cloud Function reads this token to send you your daily W at the times you chose.
  • Deletion: invalid or stale tokens are automatically removed when a push fails to deliver.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Daily W Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by the Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status.

  • We do not collect, see, or store your payment card details, billing address, or any financial information.
  • All payment processing is handled by Google Play and is governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat receives your anonymous UID and subscription status to verify entitlements. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • We store only a boolean (isPremium: true/false) in Firestore to gate features in the app.

5. Analytics and Crash Reporting

Daily W does not currently use any analytics SDK or crash reporting service beyond what Firebase provides as part of its core infrastructure (e.g. Firebase Performance, Crashlytics). No behavioural analytics or ad-targeting data is collected.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging. Privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Privacy policy.
  • Google Play — App distribution and payment processing. Privacy policy.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your Firestore data is retained as long as the app is installed. Because Daily W uses anonymous authentication, there is no account deletion flow — uninstalling the app effectively ends your session. Residual Firestore documents may remain for a period before automatic cleanup.

If you would like your data deleted manually, contact us at the address below and include your device's anonymous UID (visible in the app's Settings screen). We will delete your Firestore document within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Daily W is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has used the app and you wish for their data to be removed, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
